# OctoAcme Project Management Docs README

## Project Management Processes Overview
OctoAcme employs a structured and transparent project management methodology. The framework covers:
- **Project Initiation:** Defining objectives, stakeholders, and success criteria.
- **Project Planning:** Outlining scope, deliverables, timelines, and resource allocation.
- **Execution & Tracking:** Ongoing task management, progress tracking, and adaptability to change.
- **Risk Management & Communication:** Identifying, monitoring, and mitigating risks, along with clear and regular communication to all parties.
- **Release & Deployment:** Coordinated rollouts, quality assurance, and handoff procedures.
- **Retrospective & Continuous Improvement:** Post-project reviews and the integration of lessons learned.
- **Roles & Personas:** Designation of clear responsibilities, ensuring accountability and effective collaboration.

This approach ensures clarity, accountability, robust risk management, and continual improvement across all OctoAcme projects.
